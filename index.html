Covid-19 Solidarity Response Fund

The world is facing an unprecedented challenge with communities and economies everywhere affected by the growing COVID-19 pandemic. The world is coming together to combat the COVID-19 pandemic bringing governments, organizations from across industries and sectors and individuals together to help respond to this global outbreak. The outpouring of global solidarity and support sparked by this shared challenge has been phenomenal. Everyone can now support directly the response coordinated by CoviHelp. People and organizations who want to help fight the pandemic and support CoviHelp and partners can now donate through the COVID-Solidarity Response Fund for CoviHelp.
